**The prediction that fails.** A motion swap reverses each field's local rotation at translation onset. In this model that makes the (cued, swap) inputs *bit-for-bit identical* to (uncued, no-swap) — so the model has no choice but to predict a full reversal of the bias (right: **+33% → −25%**). The behavioral data show no such reversal. This is the model's clean falsification, and the motivation for the normalization-with-attention account we test next.
