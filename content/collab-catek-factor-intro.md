Each trial is assigned to three ±1 factors: **F1** = onset dot cue (delayed vs. non-delayed field translates); **F2** = color on translating field (delayed-onset color vs. non-delayed); **F3** = competing rotation direction (non-delayed/older vs. delayed/newer). Together these form a full 2³ factorial — all eight cells are present and the factors are orthogonal. *N* = 2,560 trials. Effects reported as full ±1 contrast (2β × 100 pp) from an OLS linear probability model.
