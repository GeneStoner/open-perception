For those curious about the underlying science, here is a selection of relevant papers organized by topic.

**Introductory / Conceptual**

- [Duncan (1984)](https://psycnet.apa.org/record/1984-32707-001) — *Selective attention and the organization of visual information.* The foundational paper establishing objects as units of attentional selection.
- [Cavanagh (2011)](https://osf.io/download/2bsn7/) — *Visual Cognition.* An accessible review of object-based attention and its relationship to visual cognition more broadly.

**Transparent Motion and Superimposed Stimuli**

- [Valdés-Sosa, Cobo & Pinilla (1998)](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/12507001_Attention_to_object_files_defined_by_transparent_motion) — *Attention to object files defined by transparent motion.* Demonstrates that transparently overlapping motion fields define distinct attentional objects.
- [Reynolds, Alborzian & Stoner (2003)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=Reynolds+Alborzian+Stoner+transparent+motion) — Transparent-motion delayed-onset studies of object-based attention.
- [Stoner & Blanc (2010)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/?term=Stoner+Blanc+object-based+attention) — Transparent-motion feature-swap studies of object-based attention.
- [Catak, Ozkan, Kafaligonul & Stoner (2022)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35398757/) — *Behavioral and ERP evidence that object-based attention utilizes fine-grained spatial mechanisms.* Cortex, 151, 55–72.

**Neural / ERP / Physiological Studies**

- [O'Craven, Downing & Kanwisher (1999)](https://www.nature.com/articles/44134) — *fMRI evidence for objects as the units of attentional selection.* Nature, 401, 584–587.
- [Hillyard, Vogel & Luck (1998)](https://royalsocietypublishing.org/doi/10.1098/rstb.1998.0281) — *Sensory gain control as a mechanism of selective attention: electrophysiological and neuroimaging evidence.*
- [Hillyard & Anllo-Vento (1998)](https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.95.3.781) — *Event-related brain potentials in the study of visual selective attention.*
- [Wannig, Rodríguez & Freiwald (2007)](https://www.nature.com/articles/nn1903) — *Attention to surfaces modulates motion processing in extrastriate area MT.* Nature Neuroscience, 10, 803–809.
- [Roelfsema (2006)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16776585/) — *Cortical algorithms for perceptual grouping.* Annual Review of Neuroscience, 29, 203–227.

**Broader Reviews**

- [Scholl (2001)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11274943/) — *Objects and attention: The state of the art.* Cognition, 80, 1–46. A comprehensive review of the field.
- [Chen (2012)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/22581274/) — *Object-based attention: A tutorial review.* Attention, Perception, & Psychophysics, 74, 784–802.
- [Frontiers Review (2016)](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/integrative-neuroscience/articles/10.3389/fnint.2016.00039/pdf) — *Mechanisms of Object-Based Attention.*
